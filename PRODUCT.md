# YumEarth EU — Product Context

## Product Purpose
European Master Distributor for YumEarth — the #1 organic candy brand. Landing page for B2B distributors and B2C health-conscious parents. Primary goals: brand credibility, product discovery, distributor outreach, and retailer leads.

## Register
brand

## Users
Two audiences:
1. **B2B** — retailers, distributors, store buyers in Europe and Middle East wanting to stock YumEarth. Need credibility, catalog, and contact.
2. **B2C** — health-conscious parents (25–45) looking for organic, allergen-free candy for their kids. Need product info, trust signals, where-to-buy.

## Brand Voice
Confident. Clean. Playful but never childish. Short sentences. No marketing fluff. Direct. The organic snack brand that doesn't look like a health food brand.

## Tone
Warm but bold. Not hippie-organic. Not sterile-health. More: "this stuff is genuinely delicious and also good for your kids."

## Anti-References
- Generic health-food green-and-white minimalism
- Childish cartoon/toy-store aesthetic
- Corporate FMCG boringness
- Excessive hero animations that slow the page
- Identical card grids

## Strategic Principles
1. Product photography is the hero — let it breathe
2. Certifications (USDA Organic, Gluten Free, Vegan, Non-GMO) build trust without being preachy
3. Distribution reach (30+ countries) signals scale and credibility
4. "No artificial colors/flavors" is the core differentiator — say it once, say it clearly
5. Speed matters: B2B buyers bounce fast

## Color Strategy
Full palette (committed). Brand uses teal (#00B2BF), lime green (#96C93D), magenta (#C45BAE), orange (#FF7803) against cream (#FFFBF0) and near-black ink (#1a1a1a). Each product line has its own accent color.

## Key Pages
- `/` Home — hero, products, story, distribution, CTA
- `/products` — full catalog with 6 product lines
- `/about` — brand story, mission, values
- `/distribution` — where to buy, B2B contact
- `/contact` — contact form
